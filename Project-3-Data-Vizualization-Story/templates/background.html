The story.  Why do you want foreclosure information?

Foreclosures are unfortunate for the people that have to go thru them, but can be a great opportunity for those that can afford them. 

Here are some key points if you want to buy foreclosures

1. Make sure you can get the cash because if you're the successful bidder, you'll have to pay the full amount within a few days. 

2. Don't bank on one property. You need to be flexible because most times you will get out bid or the foreclosure will be cancelled. 

3. Understand the risks and be comfortable with losing money. You can't see the inside, or predict when the previous owners will leave, so there are many unknowns.  

4. Use good data from reputable scraping sites like DMV Foreclosure Notices:

  A.  Only buy properties that have a good amount of equity. 

  • Properties before 2000 and during the crisis (2007 to 2009) probably have good equity. 

  B.  Buy in areas that you know. 

  C.  Get to the auction on time.
